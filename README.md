# FWS Image Prep Tool

Prepares watercolor painting images for Florida Watercolor Society show submissions.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser (double-click the file)
2. Drop your painting image onto the upload area (or click to choose a file)
3. Pick your show type: Juried or Non-Juried
4. Enter your painting title and entry number
5. Click "Prepare My Image"
6. Review the result and click "Download"

All processing happens in your browser. No images are uploaded to any server.
